# Iteration 16 - Compact news rail visibility

1. [Aesthetic] The compact news rail blended into the background; it now has a visible panel surface.
2. [Design] Its outline was indistinct at 1060px; the rail now uses a stronger boundary.
3. [Function] The rail could collapse below usable content; it now reserves 184px.
4. [Design] The compact news header consumed too much height; it now has a 36px rhythm.
5. [Aesthetic] The header divider disappeared against the stage; its rule now matches the rail boundary.
6. [Design] Header actions had uneven spacing; the compact action group now uses a 3px gap.
7. [Function] News controls were small targets in the compact rail; each now has a 28px hit area.
8. [Function] Additional news items were clipped without a route to inspect them; the list now scrolls.
9. [Aesthetic] Compact titles were visually noisy; they now use a deliberate 10px/1.4 measure.
10. [Aesthetic] Source metadata competed with titles; compact metadata now has a quieter 8px scale.
